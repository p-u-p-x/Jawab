OUR STORY

We built Jawab because small businesses deserve enterprise-grade support.

In Pakistan, millions of SME owners are the salesperson, the manager, the accountant — and the customer support rep. We built Jawab to take one of those hats off.

The cost of a missed message.

In Pakistan's booming e-commerce and services economy, Telegram is the de facto customer support channel. But most small business owners respond manually — when they remember, when they're not busy, when they're awake. A customer who doesn't get a reply in 10 minutes often doesn't come back. That's not just a missed conversation. That's missed revenue, lost trust, and a review that won't be written.

67%

of customers expect a response within the hour. Most SMEs in Pakistan have no system to guarantee it.

Jawab is the support team you couldn't afford to hire.

We built a system that classifies customer intent, generates intelligent replies using Google Gemini, and logs every ticket automatically — all in under two seconds. No agents. No shift schedules. No training costs. Just fast, accurate, always-on support that works on Telegram and the web.
